## Frequently Asked Questions

**What's the difference between salon management software and a booking app?**

Booking apps (like Calendly) just handle scheduling. Salon management software covers the full business stack: booking, POS/payments, client history, staff management, inventory, marketing, and reporting. If you're running a serious salon or spa business, you need dedicated software ‚Äî not a general-purpose scheduling tool.

**Can I accept deposits and no-show fees?**

Yes ‚Äî most platforms in this review support deposit collection and card-on-file policies for cancellation/no-show enforcement. Boulevard, Vagaro, and Mangomint have especially robust no-show protection. For solo practitioners, GlossGenius and Fresha also handle this well. No-show protection alone often pays for the software.

**Do clients need to download an app to book?**

No. All platforms reviewed here offer web-based booking ‚Äî clients can book from your website, Instagram link, or Google Business profile without downloading anything. Some platforms (Vagaro, Booksy, Fresha) also have consumer apps clients can download for convenience, but it's never required.

**What about GDPR / privacy compliance?**

If you have European clients, check each vendor's compliance posture. US-based platforms like Vagaro and Boulevard have GDPR compliance documentation available. When in doubt, ask the vendor directly and review their data processing agreements before signing up.

**Can I track hair color formulas and service notes?**

Yes ‚Äî Vagaro, Mangomint, and Boulevard all include client profile fields for formulas, notes, and service history. This is one of the most-requested salon-specific features and all three platforms handle it well. Square Appointments and Schedulicity are more limited here.

**What if I want to sell retail products?**

Vagaro, Mangomint, Boulevard, and Insight Software all have solid retail inventory management. Vagaro's retail tools are particularly strong for salons selling a significant volume of product. GlossGenius and Schedulicity are weaker on the retail/inventory side.

**Is there software for med spas specifically?**

Boulevard has become a popular choice for med spas due to its precision scheduling and premium client experience. AppFolio is also used in med spa contexts. For clinical med spa operations with complex medical charting needs, you may also want to look at dedicated med spa platforms (Aesthetic Record, Vagaro's med spa module) ‚Äî our review focuses on beauty/wellness, not medical-grade software.
