**The US marketplace is active.** Vagaro's consumer app surfaces nearby businesses to potential clients. For most US markets, the discovery volume is solid.

**Marketing automation stands out.** Automated follow-up campaigns, text marketing, and review generation are far more sophisticated than Booksy's tools.

**Memberships and recurring revenue** are well-implemented â€” easy to create, sell, and manage.

**Payroll processing** is built in, a feature Booksy doesn't offer.

**No per-booking commission.** Flat monthly fee regardless of how many clients you acquire through the platform.

- In some urban markets, Booksy has stronger marketplace penetration (particularly for barbershops).
- Monthly fee matters more if you're a part-time or very low-volume operator.

Booksy was founded in Poland in 2013 and expanded aggressively into the US market. It has built a large following among barbers and independent beauty professionals, with particular strength in major US cities.
