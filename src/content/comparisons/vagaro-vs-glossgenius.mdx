## GlossGenius Overview

GlossGenius launched in 2017 with a clear mission: build the most beautiful, easy-to-use booking software for independent beauty professionals.

### What GlossGenius Does Well

**The booking experience is stunning.** GlossGenius gives every stylist a custom, branded booking page that looks more like a boutique website than a generic booking form. Clients notice the difference.

**Onboarding is effortless.** GlossGenius is designed for stylists, not IT professionals. Most users are up and running within 30 minutes.

**The mobile app is polished.** Managing your schedule, processing payments, and communicating with clients from your phone is genuinely seamless.

**Card processing is competitive.** GlossGenius offers competitive rates with no monthly terminal fees.

**Client communication is well-designed.** Automated appointment reminders, rebooking prompts, and thank-you messages are easy to set up and look professional.

### Where GlossGenius Falls Short

- Designed primarily for solo stylists â€” managing multiple providers is a stretch.
- No inventory management.
